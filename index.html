About us




From the University of Babylon College of Information Technology Student Fatima Hassan Yaser initiated the initiative entitled "Iraqi IT Women" whose goal is to educate girls in the field of IT, In short it's special intensive training workshop for girls to enter the world of IT,fought and challenged a lot to get to Baghdad and the establishment of the first educational team of young girls formed a professional team in the field of information technology and set up the training workshop to expand the scope of girls in the field of information technology as the first project to educate girls as a category misleading in this area
